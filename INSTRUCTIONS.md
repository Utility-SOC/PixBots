# PixelBots Instructions

## Controls

### Gameplay
- **W, A, S, D**: Move Player
- **Mouse Cursor**: Aim
- **Left Click**: Shoot
- **V** or **C**: Open Component Viewer (Inventory/Loadout)
- **Q**: Open Equipment Menu
- **K**: Open Crafting Menu
- **R**: Open Reactor Debug Menu
- **I**: Open Debug Spawn Menu
- **J**: Open Debug Biome Switcher
- **ESC**: Pause / Back / Close Menu

### Component Viewer
- **Left/Right Arrows**: Cycle through equipped components
- **E**: Edit the currently selected component (Opens Hex Editor)
- **ESC**: Close Viewer

### Hex Editor
- **Left Click**: Place selected tile
- **Right Click**: Rotate tile (if applicable)
- **Shift + Right Click**: Delete tile
- **1-7**: Select Tile from Palette
    1. Conduit
    2. Amplifier
    3. Resonator
    4. Splitter
    5. Reflector
    6. Weapon Mount
    7. Super Conduit
- **ESC**: Save and Close

### Debug Menus
- **Debug Spawn (I)**: Follow on-screen prompts (Number keys) to spawn items, enemies, or cores.
- **Debug Biome (J)**: Press 1-7 to switch biomes instantly.

## Mechanics
- **Energy Flow**: Energy is generated by your Core (Torso) and flows through your components.
- **Synergies**: Combine different energy types to create powerful effects.
    - **Vortex**: Pulls enemies together and smashes them for extra damage.
    - **Lightning**: Chains damage to nearby enemies.
    - **Fire**: Burns enemies over time.
    - **Ice**: Slows enemies down.
- **Hex Grid**: Optimize your components by arranging tiles to maximize flow efficiency and activate synergies.
